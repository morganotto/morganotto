Digital Narrative, 2022


The "Digital Narrative" project is an experimental web-coding project that explores the extensive abilities of web-coding through animation, typography, and interactability.

The narrative being explored is the abstract concept of intrusive thoughts, more specifically regarding weather. The page is built to be consumed aesthetically as a web-design exploration rather than for functional use.

programs used: sublime developing software

Mid-Century Modernism 2021


The "Mid-Century Modernism" project is an expansive study of the design themes of the midcentury (1950-65). Led by thorough research, this project began with a zine titled "The Mix", which portrayed design strategies of the mid-century period through popular music of the period.

Following this zine, an app was developed that showcased mid-century modernist furniture in an abstract, unique way, breaking away from the traditional formatting of UX/UI design.

The purpose of this project was to combine contemporary design ideals with that of a previous time period. In combining these two styles, a new form of design is created that portrays a contemporary feel with nostalgic symbols.

programs used: adobe illustrator, photoshop, indesign, figma
