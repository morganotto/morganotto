Morgan Otto is a Los Angeles based graphic designer with an emphasis in digital formats: web design/development, UX/UI design. She is also experienced in print/publication design, and takes interest in other artistic mediums such as photography, printmaking, and textile design. Her practice is driven by social justice, sustainability, and contemporary design influences. Her work approaches the intersection between physical and digital design, bringing elements of materiality and texture into the digital realm to communicate with emotional impact. Otto is currently attending Otis College of Art and Design, expected graduation date: July 2024.

Her design manifesto is included under 'portfolio'.
